About me

Hey! My name is Oliver, I am a 16 year old developer and go by the alias 'Nightmare' online. I started my programming saga back in 2017 learning the JavaScript programming lanugage by creating Discord bots. I started off small, creating simple reactions and responses that ran when detecting a specific word or phrase in a users message. Years later, I still endulge in programming Discord bots, but have also gone onto learning new programming languages like C# and Assembly.


What projects are you currently working on?

Currently, I only have one project for an upcoming Discord bot, which goes by the name 'Kenji', more about that can be found on the 'Projects' page. Other than Kenji, I have no other ongoing projects, however I have lots planned for the future. My plans include: AI, Game Development and more.


What are your hobbies and interests?

Other than programming, music is a big part of my life, I enjoy just listening to music when bored, or working on my projects. My favourite artists and bands are Mitski, Radiohead and Arctic Monkeys, however I do enjoy a lot of artists other than those few.
